Chicken Korma

Description

This is an easy recipe for chicken korma. It's not too spicy, but has lots of flavor. If you have ever wanted to try Indian food, this is a good one to start with. Serve over hot rice.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 6 tablespoons plain yoghurt
  • 2 tablespoons mango chutney

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 2-quart baking dish.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat. Cook onions in oil until soft.
  3. Place onion, yogurt, mango chutney, garlic, turmeric, chile powder, and garam masala into the bowl of a food processor. Process into smooth sauce; it should be about the consistency of thick cream. Add a bit of water or yogurt to thin it if needed. Spread chicken into prepared baking dish, and pour onion sauce over chicken.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through. Sprinkle sliced almonds over the top, and serve.
